Firefly and Veracross unite, creating a powerful school management system

Firefly and Veracross are jointly committed to enhancing the educational experience for students, teachers, and support staff. As part of the Veracross family, Firefly is excited to support schools through an integrated school MIS platform that combines the best student behaviour and engagement tools with the best school management system.

Together, Firefly and Veracross offer a comprehensive solution for schools across every key department: from admissions to academic management accounting, fundraising, fees management, online learning, parent engagement, and so much more.
